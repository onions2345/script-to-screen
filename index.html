Script to Screen

Turn ANY Script / Screenplay into a movie FREE

Paste a script. Gemini works out who speaks each line and casts a voice, reads it aloud, and sketches the visual beats — then stitches the voices into one audio track and makes your video. Runs entirely in your browser.

No key yet? Get a free one from Google AI Studio — sign in with Google, click "Create API key," paste it here. Each visitor uses their own key; keys can't be handed out by a login button.
Your key stays in this browser only (saved to localStorage on this device). Never commit it to the repo — GitHub Pages is public. For safety, restrict the key to your Pages URL in Google Cloud Console → Credentials → HTTP referrers.

Saved projects

Saved on this device, in this browser — your script, voices and images are kept so you can close the tab and come back. Clearing browser data (or using another device) removes them.

Audio generation

Google Cloud TTS uses the same Google key as Gemini but is a separate service with its own (large, free-tier) quota — the best way around the Gemini 100/day TTS cap. One-time setup: in Google Cloud Console, enable the "Cloud Text-to-Speech API" on the same project as your key. It auto-assigns a Neural2 voice per character.

All the Puter options are free and need no key (a quick Puter sign-in may pop up on first use) and don't touch your Gemini quota. "Puter · Gemini" keeps your exact cast voices; the others use their own voices, auto-assigned per character (ElevenLabs uses one voice for all). StreamElements is also free with no key and no sign-in (the classic "Brian" stream voices), though it's an unofficial endpoint that can be busy at times. The Voice model dropdown only shows for the Gemini-voice options.

Video & image generation

The image model draws the visuals used in the storyboard and video. Lower resolution makes the rendered video smaller.

STEP 1

Your script

Any format works — labelled (NARRATOR: / ON SCREEN:) or plain prose. Gemini figures out the structure.

STEP 2

Breakdown & casting

Each character is cast to a Gemini voice. Change any of them, then generate.

Long script? The free tier caps requests per minute, so some lines/images may fail at first — that's normal. Wait a few seconds and click again; it only fills what's missing. Repeat until done.
